This is a story about Jake. Jake loves videogames, sometimes he plays so many that he never has time for other things! This is a big problem. Recently jake has decided that he wants to change this. So Jake decided to delete all his games! However, one of them refuses to be deleted properly, and Jake doesnt know what to do. Finally, Jake decides to reset his computer, but when he does, he is sucked into his computer, his fate unknown...
